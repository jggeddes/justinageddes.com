History of Cocoa

Chocolate started in the Mexico and Guatemala region. We have beverages that were made from chocolate that date back from 450 B.C. Chocolate as we know it today wasn't your average candy bar it was a drink. Back then chocolate was used as a drink. Even though chocolate is still used as a drink today we can find it in a candy store in the form of a candy bar.

Cocoa trees grow in Mesoamerica. These trees are where we get our chocolate from. The tree produces these beans in what is called a sheath. The bean may have been fermented and to be used in an alcoholic beverage back then.
